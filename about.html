About Me!

Hey! My name is Alex Gada, and I am a full stack software developer. Not that there is a conventional route into this field, but if there was one mine certainly wasn't it.

After graduating University with a first class degree in Psychology I decided to try become a clinical psychologist, and so for the first 2 and a half years after University I was working on the frontline of mental-health support work to gain experience for my doctorate.

Despite working throughout the pandemic, it gave me time to reflect about my career and where I wanted to be in 10 years. I decided, for my own sake, that having a job you love is the most important career decision you can make. I've always so I researched how I could get into software development. This is where I found Northcoders and I haven't looked back.

I've loved every second of coding since I started (although my eyes may beg to differ). I love that all you need is a screen and a keyboard and you can make something that someone might use every day and I am so excited to get my first job in the industry to start my journey.

Outside of coding my Dad has successfully completed his lifelong goal of getting me into golf, and with a 1 year old pup my free time is spent walking on the course with my dad or my dog talking me for a walk!
